float ui explorations

date: 9-13-20

motivation and criteria

I am currently interning at Float, a company aiming to connect hosts with renters looking for monthly parking. As a UX/UI intern, I am working to redesign the entire website interface, to create a more appealing and user friendly experience for Float's customers. I am working with another intern Krupa Patel, and we are currently conducting user research and in the process of building out the site as low fidelity wireframes. We are constructing the information architecture and adding many features to our designs that are not incorporated in the current site. Our goal is to prioritize the user and generate a design that is efficient and memorable for both renters and hosts using the site. Later this fall, I will be updating my site with a case study detailing the entire UX process. For the UI, we have created a variety of explorations and elements to help build a concrete brand for Float, and I thought I would share these as a precursor to the final UX designs.
