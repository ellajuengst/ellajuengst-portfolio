branding kappa theta pi

motivation and criteria

I recently joined the executive board of Kappa Theta Pi, UNC Chapel Hill's professional technology fraternity. I am serving as a Co-Director of Marketing. I am in charge of desinging the fraternity's branding, logos, website, and merchandise. The fraternity represents a mix of professional and social opportunities for students at UNC. I wanted the branding to reflect a clean, modern, and sophisticated style while retaining some fun, playful elements.
