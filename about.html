about

I grew up in Arlington, Virginia and graduated from H-B Woodlawn Secondary Program in 2018. I am currently studying at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, pursuing a BS in Computer Science with a minor in Studio Art. My expected graduation date is May 2022.

I have always been passionate about art and design, and I grew up taking many art courses in and outside of school. I have studied color theory, figure drawing, and principles of design. I love working with both traditional and digital mediums. I have experience in Adobe Creative Cloud applications including Photoshop, Illustrator, and XD.

Throughout my whole life, I have also loved using critical thinking to solve mathematical problems. Since I came to college, I have discovered my interest in computer science. In terms of programming languages, I have experience in Java, HTML5/CSS3/Sass, Bootstrap, TypeScript, C, and Assembly.
